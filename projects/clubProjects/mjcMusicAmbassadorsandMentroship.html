Problem: Lack of access to music instruction, instruments, and private instruction in areas of music that are of most interest to community youth; and lack of opportunities to explore and become knowledgeable about possible educational & career paths in music.

Solution: We have developed partnerships and collaborations with several non-profits and local businesses to provide music education, music therapy services, performance opportunities, and event organization and support to those in the community who have limited access to these resources. The community benefits from the increased presence and interaction with music and the arts and our students get the professional experience as music educators, music therapists and various roles in music technology and industry. This exploration will help them clarify their educational and career goals.
