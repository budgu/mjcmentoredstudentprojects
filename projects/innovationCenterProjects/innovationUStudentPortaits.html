Student: Thelma Saldivar

Mentors: Jeremy Wilson (Engineering, MJC) and Debbie Gilbert (English, MJC)

Problem: We wanted prospective Innovation students as well as community partners to know about the significance of Invention Education for students. Understanding and imagining solutions to community problems is empowering and potentially transformative for both participants and the community.

Solution: Jeremy and Debbie thought of Thelma and her photographic project to highlight the value refugees bring to the community. We asked her to take and combine portraits of our students with their words. Her portraits make clear that Innovation U students feel empowered and enriched; they know that with empathy, analysis and innovative thinking, they can make their world a better place.
