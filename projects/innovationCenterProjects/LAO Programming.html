LAO Youth Programming

Student: Jonah Olsen et al

Mentors: Jacquelyn Forte, Jeremy Wilson, Travis Silvers, Debbie Gilbert

Problem: The "college fear factor" and general unfamiliarity with higher education are very real challenges that prevent many community youths from envisioning themselves as future college students.

Solution: Bringing students on campus for fun, low-stakes activities, during which they can meet other students just like them, can help break down those fear factor barriers and increase familiarity with the college experience. Through the generous sponsorship of the Raymus Foundation, whom provided materials, MJC music and engineering club students were paid through the Solve CC funds to work with members of the Legacy Alliance Outreach program's after school youth, offering multiple sessions of car kit building and guitar lessons.
