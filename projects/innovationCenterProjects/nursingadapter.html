Nursing Port Adapter

Students: Joseph Pomerantz

Mentors: Eurico Silva (Machine Shop, MJC), Jeremy Wilson (Engineering, MJC) and Debbie Gilbert (English, MJC)

Problem: A major expense and roadblock for the nursing students of MJC are the lumen adapter ports used for their practice dummies. Lumen adapters range from hundreds to thousands of dollars to replace, and so the nursing students often have to make do with worn down and broken equipment.

Solution: After a visit to the MJC Innovation Center, Juan Garcia requested that a student technician tackle this issue and see if there was any way to recreate the needed tools in house. After trial and error and consulting with Eurico Silva and Jeremy Wilson, the technician was able to recreate the port as a 3D printable model only costing $1.30 to print.
