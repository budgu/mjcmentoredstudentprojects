Projects Launched from Classes

Makerspace Instrument Project

Problem: To explore the construction, tuning and performance of handmade instruments.

Soap Dispenser

Problem: The Shower Shuttle, the mobile shower service that brings hot showers to the region’s homeless population had an issue with their shampoo dispensers. The lids were being bumped off during use and cracking after hitting the floor of the shower. The lids are not available individually, so it was necessary to purchase new dispensers each time a lid cracked.

Exhuming Local History

Problem: Local history in our community is very limited. We have a few places where parts of our story is told, but it isn’t very comprehensive. Our project is the beginning of a long-term term effort to fill in the gaps in our knowledge of our collective past.
